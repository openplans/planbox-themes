What information is collected?

Location, Category

Participants choose a category and place a pin on the map, marking the location of their feedback.

Housing Business Streets/Parking Walking/Biking Public Safety Parks/Rivers Public Space Other

Title, Description

Thoughts are shared as freeform text.

Name, Email, Demographics

Participants leave their name, and can optionally provide private information: email address, ZIP code, age, and race/ethnicity.
